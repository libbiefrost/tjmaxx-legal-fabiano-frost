What Happened

The incident at Machesney Park TJ Maxx

On February 28, 2026, a customer discovered a video camera hidden inside the women's dressing room of the TJ Maxx store in Machesney Park. The Winnebago County Sheriff's Office investigated and identified the store manager, Jeffrey Wallin, as the suspect. He was charged with unauthorized video recording, live video transmission, and obstructing justice. According to attorneys now representing victims, TJ Maxx may have had knowledge that recording was occurring at the store.

The legal angle

TJ Maxx may bear responsibility

Attorneys representing victims assert that TJ Maxx, as a corporation, had a duty to protect customers and may have been aware of the conduct — opening the door to civil claims beyond the criminal case.

Your rights

This is a serious violation

Illinois law protects individuals from unauthorized video surveillance in private spaces. Victims may be entitled to compensation for the emotional harm, loss of privacy, and distress caused by this breach.
